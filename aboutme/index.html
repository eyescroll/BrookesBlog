Welcome to my page.

A little information about me...

Currently studying BA Publishing at university, to potentially follow with an MA in the field. Eventually, the aim is entering into either children's literature or science-fiction and fantasy publishing. This is because these are the audiences mostly likely to get excited about books and the sharing of stories, which is why storytelling began. To entertain and teach.

(Coincidentally, these are also the two literary areas most likely to feature dragons, which I am incredibly fond of.)

Language collector

I collect interesting and unusual words. My current favourites are psithurism and coddiwomple.

Most recent read was fantasy-adventure A Natural History of Dragons by Marie Brennan. However, one of my favourite books is Dianne Wynne Jone's Howl's Moving Castle.

Typically read fantasy and sci-fi novels, but am making an effort to read more non-fiction, beginning with How to Be A Victorian by Ruth Goodman. Yet this may be delayed once Skin Curse is released next month.

Three of the best dragons are:

  • Draco, from the 1996 Dragonheart film, voiced by Sean Connery. (I recommend you go (re)watch it. Now.)
  • Nethore, from SaoiMarie's Rider's Series on Wattpad.
  • Temeraire, from Naomi Novik's eponymous series.

I am currently working up the courage to sew together my latest crochet project, an amigurumi dragon that has taken over six months thus far.

The current challenges in my life are my degree, teaching a flatmate to cook, and working on writing projects.
